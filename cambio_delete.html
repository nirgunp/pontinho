Delete your Cambio account

How to request permanent account and data removal.

  1. Send an email from the email address linked to your Cambio account to spellmeierlucian@gmail.com.
  2. In the message, ask to remove your Cambio account (for example: “Please delete my Cambio account.”).
  3. After we confirm the request, your account and all related data will be deleted.

Tip: You can also delete your account from inside the app under Profile, if that option is available on your device.
